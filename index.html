Welcome to the home page of Fro Games! This is where update logs will be and what the latest updates are on the game Beneath. Beneath is a single-player horror game where you, the player go through emotions of others in your life and those emotions of negativity come after you and hunt you down. You experience the life and sadness of others and what really happened to people and you must fix it and reverse everything back to life as it was.

The game will be first releasing on Roblox and then later on releasing as a game of its own. Beneath is in development by Fro Games.
